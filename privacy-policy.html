Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Pocket Palace collects absolutely no personal data. Everything you create stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

What We Collect

Nothing. Pocket Palace does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

All content you create in Pocket Palace — your memory palaces, rooms, loci, PAO lists, and tracker history — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using Apple's standard data persistence. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is never accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be exported by you at any time via the CSV export feature in Settings
  • Is deleted when you delete the app

In-App Purchases

Pocket Palace Premium is a one-time non-consumable in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not handle, store, or have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, crash reporters, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

Third-Party Services

Pocket Palace loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) when an internet connection is available. This is a standard web request that may log your IP address per Google's standard infrastructure logging. No personal information is associated with this request. Future versions of Pocket Palace will bundle fonts locally to eliminate this entirely.

Children's Privacy

Pocket Palace does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Given that we collect no data, we do not anticipate meaningful changes.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at: privacy@pocketpalace.app

You can also find Pocket Palace on the App Store — search "Pocket Palace memory."
